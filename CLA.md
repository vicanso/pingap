# Pingap Contributor License Agreement (CLA)

Thank you for your interest in contributing to Pingap! To protect the project, our users, and you as a contributor, we require a simple Contributor License Agreement (CLA). 

This agreement clarifies the intellectual property rights of your contributions, ensuring that Pingap can be safely distributed and maintained in the future under its open-source license.

By contributing to Pingap, you agree to the following terms:

1. **You Retain Copyright:** You retain full ownership and copyright of your original contributions. This agreement does not take away your right to use your own code elsewhere.
2. **License Grant:** You grant the Pingap project a perpetual, worldwide, non-exclusive, no-charge, royalty-free, irrevocable right and license to use, copy, modify, merge, publish, and distribute your contributions under the project's current open-source license (e.g., Apache License 2.0).
3. **Original Work:** You declare that your contribution is your original creation and that you have the legal right to grant this license. If your employer holds the rights to your code, you confirm you have their permission to contribute it.

*By checking the CLA box in the Pull Request template, you officially indicate your acceptance of these terms.*
